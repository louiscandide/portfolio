DRIP DYNASTY

Back

Project Description

Drip Dynasty is an online store offering a variety of sport-focused clothing and footwear brands. The site is divided into two distinct parts: client and administrator.
Client Part:
  • Login Page: Users can access the login page to log into their existing account.
  • Create an Account: Users also have the option to create a new account by accessing the "Create an Account" page.
  • My Account: Once logged in, clients can access the "My Account" page. On this page, they have the option to delete their account if needed.
  • Catalog: Clients can explore the catalog page, which lists all available products. They can filter products according to their preferences.
  • Product Details: By clicking on a specific product, clients access a detailed product page. They can add the product to their cart from this page.
Admin Part:
  • Login Page: Administrators have a dedicated login page to access their administrator account.
  • Catalog: Administrators can access the catalog page to add, modify, or delete products. This gives them complete control over the product catalog management.
  • Order History: Administrators also have access to an order history page, where they can view the history of orders placed on the platform.
The site's architecture provides a smooth user experience for clients and provides administrators with the tools needed to effectively manage the product catalog and track order history.
